Can I syncronize my data across devices?

A data sync feature is comming soon.

I have a new device (iPhone, iPad or iPod touch). How do I carry over my progress?

All your Benkyō data is backup-up with your device backup. Therefore it carries over when you carry the backup over to your new device.
To learn more on how to backup your iPhone, iPad or iPod touch, go to: https://support.apple.com/HT203977

How do I manage my subscription?

You can manage (cancel or change plan) your subscription at any time in the App Store, go to: Account (top right corner) → subscriptions → Benkyō.
You can also change your plan (for example switch from monthly to annual or lifetime) within the app: on the Benkyō Pro page (tap the more button then 'See products available for purchase'). If you go Lifetime, do not forget to cancel your subscription on the App Store.

How do I switch from a Benkyō Pro subscription to Lifetime?

Easy!
In the Benkyō Pro page: tap the more button (three dots) → 'See products available for purchase' → Lifetime
Do not forget to cancel your subscription on the App Store: Account (top right corner) → subscriptions → Benkyō.
