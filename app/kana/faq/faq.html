I have a new device (iPhone, iPad or iPod touch). How do I carry over my progress?

All your data is saved with your device backup. Therefore it is restored along with the backup to your new device.
To learn more on how to backup your iPhone, iPad or iPod touch, go to: https://support.apple.com/HT203977
